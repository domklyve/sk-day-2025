Keynote Speaker

Dr. Jenny Quinn

Executive Director of the Seattle Universal Math Museum (SUMM) and past President of the Mathematical Association of America (2021–2022), Dr. Quinn is a nationally recognized teacher, author, and scholar whose mission is to make mathematics accessible, appreciated, and humane.

At SUMM, she leads joyful, hands‑on math experiences for communities across the region, with hundreds of events yearly and a public lecture series on the way.

Solving Mathematical Mysteries invites everyone to be a curious, creative, and courageous mathematical detective.

About SUMM

The Seattle Universal Math Museum aims to spark each and every person to love math through interactive exhibits and community programming.

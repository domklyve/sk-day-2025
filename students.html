Activity Descriptions

  • Virtual Reality and the Space Station — Explore the ISS in VR and try a spacewalk mission.
  • Ready, Set, Bet! — Simulate horse‑race betting and experience probability in action.
  • Draw the Line: Discovering Euler Circuits — Solve the Königsberg Bridge problem and more.
  • Coloring to Resolve Conflict — Map coloring and conflict‑free regionings.
  • More than just a Number — Encode personal data using Soundex and compute an IL driver’s license number.
  • Cut your way to the Stars — One‑cut paper artistry and geometric symmetry.

Combined Activity Blocks
